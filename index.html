About Our Program

Our Mission

The Pathfinder Regional Robotics Program aims to inspire and empower students through hands-on robotics experience. We develop technical skills, critical thinking, and teamwork while providing opportunities for students to apply classroom knowledge to real-world engineering challenges.

Our Community

We create a supportive environment where students of all experience levels can contribute and grow. Our school brings together students from 9 different towns, letting them collaborate, problem-solve, and celebrate achievements as they represent Pathfinder Tech!

Our Robotics Programs

FIRST Robotics Competition (FRC)

Our FRC team designs and builds industrial-sized robots to compete in high-intensity competitions. Students gain experience with advanced control systems, programming, and mechanical design while working with mentors from industry professionals.

Learn More

VEX Robotics Competition

Our VEX Robotics program engages students in designing and building robots using the VEX platform. This accessible entry point to competitive robotics helps develop foundational skills in mechanics, electronics, and programming through year-round competitions.
