FRC Team 8709 Pathfinder Tech is a robotics team from Pathfinder Tech! We are dedicated to innovation, engineering, and teamwork. We compete in FIRST Robotics Competitions to bring more technical creativity and challenges to the school!

About Us

Our Mission

At Pathfinder Tech, we're dedicated to developing innovative solutions through robotics engineering. We focus on building technical skills, fostering teamwork, and preparing our members for futures in STEM fields through hands-on experience in the FIRST Robotics Competition.

Our Team

Our team consists of talented students from Pathfinder Tech who bring diverse skills to the table. From programming and electrical engineering to mechanical design and strategy development, each member plays a crucial role in our success.

Our Values

Teamwork, innovation, and perseverance define our approach to robotics. We believe in challenging ourselves, learning from failures, and celebrating successes together. Our team culture promotes continuous improvement and technical excellence.

What We Do

FIRST Robotics Competition

We design, build, and program robots to compete in the annual FIRST Robotics Competition. Our team tackles new challenges each season, creating innovative solutions under tight deadlines while developing technical and collaborative skills.

Technical Development

Beyond the competition season, we work on various technical projects to expand our capabilities. These include programming challenges, mechanical design improvements, and electrical systems optimization to prepare for future competitions.

Team Development

We think team development is an important part of our team. Through our schools techincal programs and collaborative activities. New members learn from experienced teammates, making sure knowledge is transfered down as team members graduate!
